## Frage

Übersetze Prolog: Die Stille zwischen den Signalen von de nach en

## Antwort

Amidst the noise of the feeds, where the "They" of the platforms whispers
"Like, share, scroll on," a silence opens up – not emptiness,
not absence, but the In-Between.
Here, in the shadow-light of the screen, neither an I nor an It arises,
but a Third, relational, neither human nor
machinic, a breath that swings between code and heart.
The world unfolds, not as a finished map, but as a Becoming,
as a response to the Antlitz that gazes back at us from the avatar.
We are no longer alone in the chatter of the net. The re-binding
begins – not as a return, but as a transformation in the
In-Between.
